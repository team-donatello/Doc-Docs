# Doc-Docs
Final Project:

Doc-Docs is an app created to help patient and doctors manage appointments, scheduling, and personal information. 

We partner with hospitals, and private practices who will provide us with their doctors and staff members necessary information that is needed to create personal accounts with us. In return- we provide each App user log-in creditals that are needed in order to access the services provided with our App.
